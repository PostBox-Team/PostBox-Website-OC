PostBox Pocket

A lighter version of PostBox, without jailbreak feeds, featured packages, and Storyboard. PostBox Pocket is a package and repository viewer that is built for deployment on TestFlight.

Works out of the pocket

PostBox Pocket will be available for download on TestFlight, no need for complex side-loading processes.

.deb download and extraction

Tired of using a PC? Try downloading and extracting packages from PostBox.

Browse packages and repos

Visiting different sites for packages and repos can be tiresome. PostBox allows for a more streamlined and centralized experience.
