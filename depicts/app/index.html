PostBox, not a package manager.

PostBox brings the latest jailbreak news to your fingertips. Use PostBox to access your favorite packages and repositories while unjailbroken, all without the hassle of constantly searching on the web. With in-app .deb download and extraction, you can install themes using only your phone.

Keep reading to see what PostBox has to offer!

.deb download and extraction

Tired of using a PC? Try downloading and extracting packages from PostBox. For jailed users, this means you can download and apply your favorite jailbreak themes straight from your device!

A pool of feeds

Enjoy a lively feed of various sources to always stay connected with the jailbreak scene, featuring r/jailbreak, r/iOSthemes, and iDownloadBlog. Sources are updated in real time so you don't miss out.

Package rating and review

Leave feedback on packages and let other users know the tips and experiences of using them.

Add and manage repos

PostBox contains over 200 repos for efficient repo finding. Effortlessly look for information that will previously require painstaking work for if you are not jailbroken.

Want more information? Check out those links to learn more about PostBox.
